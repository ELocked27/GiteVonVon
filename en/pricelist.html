Prices

Booking & Pricing

The tarrif is €75.00 per night, the minimum stay is two nights.
For guests wishing to enjoy an extended stay, we offer a special weekly rate: when booking seven nights, you only pay for six nights at €75.00 each, with the seventh night offered free of charge. This brings the total cost for a full week to €450.00.
The price includes bed linen, towels, and final cleaning, ensuring that you can relax and fully enjoy your stay without any additional housekeeping concerns.
Please note that the local tourist tax is not included in the price and must be paid on-site, in accordance with local regulations.
